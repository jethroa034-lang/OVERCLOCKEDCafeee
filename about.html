About Overclocked Café

Overclocked Café (Overclocked Coffee) is a specialty coffee shop located in Laoag City, Ilocos Norte. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, artisan coffee in a warm and creative space where people can gather, work, or simply relax.

Founded by passionate coffee lovers, we blend craftsmanship and community. From carefully selected beans to hand-crafted brews, every cup is made with intention.

We are open daily from 8 AM to 11 PM, offering you a place to start your morning or wind down your night.
